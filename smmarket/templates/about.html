ABOUT US




WE PROVIDE YOU FRESH AND ORGANIC FOOD


Organic foods are grown either by natural means, without chemical fertilizers or obtained from nature. Such farming methods can produce organic foods in a fairly clean and safe manner and are also very beneficial for human health. Organic foods are free from any type of chemical fertilizers and pesticides. In addition, organic fertilizers are used in place of chemical fertilizers and mainly these fertilizers are used only in organic farming.



ABOUT ORGANIC FARMING


In many countries, only organic farming is legally recognized, which is determined by the International Federation of Organic Agriculture Movements. Any food sold as organic food in India has to meet national organic food production standards, which are governed by the Agricultural and Processed Food Products Development Authority.
The biggest feature of organic diet is that they do not have any side effects on the body. No chemical, pesticides, drugs or preservatives have been used in it. It contains essential elements like vitamins, minerals, protein, calcium, iron etc. These are the things that keep the body healthy.
